About TVETPrime

Digitizing TVET for a Skilled, Tech-Ready Kenya

At TVETPrime, we are redefining how technical and vocational training institutions operate.

Our Mission

We transform TVET education through cutting-edge technology, data-driven systems, and digital learning solutions that prepare learners for the demands of modern industries.

Our Vision

We envision a Kenya where every technical institution is equipped with technology that enhances skills training, boosts innovation, and drives national development. TVETPrime is your trusted partner in making that vision a reality.

We believe

We believe that every TVET institution deserves access to affordable, innovative, and locally relevant digital tools — because the future of work starts in the classroom.
